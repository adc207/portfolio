MIAX Exchange Internship

During my internship with MIAX Exchange Group, I contributed to a trading systems development team where I worked on a TOM (Top of Market) application. My main responsibility was to build a system that took in the Best Bid and Offer (BBO) values after trade matching and broadcast them to other exchanges. This required careful handling of market data protocols and working closely with C++ systems to maintain ultra-low latency. I collaborated with previous interns' matching engine code and built atop it in a modular, efficient way.
